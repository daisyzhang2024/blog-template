## Ingredients

- Yogurt
- Blueberries
- Granola
- Banana

## Instructions

1. Put 1 serving size worth of yogurt in bowl.
2. Top with blueberries, sliced banana, and granola!

## Tips & Notes

- Eat with whole wheat bread or eggs!
