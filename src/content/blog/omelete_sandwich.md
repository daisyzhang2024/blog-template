## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- Pita bread
- cheese
- green pepper

## Instructions

1. Oil pan, put mixed eggs on a coat pita bread
2. Add mixed cheese, green pepper
3. Flip into omlete shape

## Tips & Notes

- Serve with fresh veggies
